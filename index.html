About Me

Hello! I'm Diya Fathima, a Bachelor of Architecture student with a passion for designing spaces that tell stories. Based in Kerala, India, I am inspired by the region's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. My journey in architecture is driven by a fascination with how spaces can shape human emotions and experiences, motivating me to create environments that inspire and connect.

As a budding architect, I am eager to blend technical skills with creative vision, crafting designs that are both functional and meaningful.

My Design Philosophy

  • Sustainability is a responsibility.
  • Innovation through problem-solving.
  • Human-centric design.
  • Beauty in functionality.
